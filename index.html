System Capabilities

True Portability

Runs entirely from a single EXE. Saves data locally next to the executable. Perfect for USB drives and cloud sync.

Controller First

Navigate the entire UI with your gamepad. Designed for couch gaming, no keyboard or mouse required.

Live Dashboard

Real-time clock, date, and auto-detected local weather widget. Your hub for everything, not just a launcher.

Custom Library

Add games, customize box art, and rearrange tiles with "Swap" mode. Make your collection truly yours.

Immersive Audio

Full support for background music (BGM) and UI sound effects. Get into the zone before you even start playing.

Integrated News

Stay updated with a built-in RSS feed reader tailored for gaming news. Read the latest headlines instantly.
